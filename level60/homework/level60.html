Top Attractions

Tbilisi Old Town

Stroll through the charming cobblestone streets of Tbilisi, filled with colorful houses, historic churches, and vibrant cafes.

Svetitskhoveli Cathedral

Visit this UNESCO World Heritage Site in Mtskheta, a masterpiece of Georgian architecture and spirituality.

Kazbegi Mountains

Hike or ski in the breathtaking Caucasus Mountains, home to stunning vistas and the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church.

Rich Culture & Cuisine

Georgia is a land of ancient traditions, from polyphonic singing to world-famous winemaking. Savor khachapuri, khinkali, and wines crafted over 8,000 years. Experience the warmth of Georgian hospitality and vibrant festivals.
